Things to know when running LickBuilder:

Page 1: Simply click the button to start the program.
		The red 'X' will ask if you're sure before exiting the program.
		
Page 2: Selection of Difficulty Level 
		clicking on the pictures will give you examples of the kinds of rudiments you will see at each difficulty level
			child forms: clicking on these pictures will not do anything, they are simply visual examples
			
		drop down menu to select difficulty level: 
			'loading' button will appear 
			clicking 'OK' will open the form that contains the picture boxes of the musical representation of output
